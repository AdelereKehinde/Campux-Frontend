Packed with Powerful Features

CampusCore brings together every tool a student needs into one seamless, intuitive platform.

Real-time Messaging
Send messages, share files, and collaborate with classmates in real time — individually or in groups.
Campus Social Feed
Share updates, announcements, and moments with your campus community through a rich social timeline.
Marketplace
Buy, sell, and rent gadgets, services, student businesses, and more in your campus marketplace.
Ads & Boost System
Promote posts and listings with our boost system to reach more students across campus.
Smart Student Profiles
Showcase your skills, achievements, and interests with a rich, customizable student profile.
AI Study Assistant
Get instant help with coursework, summarize notes, and generate study guides powered by AI.
Events & Calendar
Discover campus events, set reminders, and manage your schedule from one place.
Internship & Opportunities
Browse internships, scholarships, and career opportunities curated for your campus community.
Study Groups
Create or join study groups by course, department, or interest. Learn better, together.
Smart Notifications
Stay updated with intelligent, contextual push notifications — never miss what matters.
Role-Based Access
Students, lecturers, clubs, and admins each get tailored experiences and permissions.
Reputation System
Earn badges and credibility points through helpful contributions, verified sales, and ratings.
Admin Dashboard
Powerful tools for administrators to manage users, content, and campus-wide announcements.
Content Moderation
AI-powered moderation ensures a safe, respectful, and productive campus environment.
Analytics
Deep insights into engagement, reach, and performance for individuals, clubs, and businesses.
Unified Content System
Posts, marketplace listings, and ads seamlessly integrated into one unified content experience.
